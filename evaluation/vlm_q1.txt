The purpose of the NIST AI Risk Management Framework 1.0 (AI RMF 1.0) is to provide a structured approach to minimize potential negative impacts of AI systems while maximizing positive impacts. It aims to help organizations address the risks associated with AI systems, leading to more trustworthy AI. The framework includes specific functions—GOVERN, MAP, MEASURE, and MANAGE—to guide organizations in managing AI risks throughout the AI lifecycle. It is designed to be flexible, allowing for the adaptation to new risks as they emerge, and to foster greater awareness of existing standards and best practices for managing AI risks.
