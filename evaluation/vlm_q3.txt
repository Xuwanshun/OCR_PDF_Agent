The inflation chart in the Federal Reserve Monetary Policy Report indicates that inflation has moderated further, moving closer to the Federal Open Market Committee's longer-run objective of 2 percent. Specifically, the price index for personal consumption expenditures (PCE) rose 2.6 percent over the 12 months ending in December, down from 2.7 percent the previous year and significantly lower than its peak of 7.2 percent in mid-2022.
